Aeronautical Meteorological Services

The South African Weather Service provide meteorological information to the Aviation industry in South Africa through ICAO regulations and specifications in accordance with Annexure 3 and local user agreements.

Information is compiled and distributed by the Aviation Weather Centre at O.R Tambo International Airport and main forecasting centres at Bloemfontein, Cap Town, Durban and Port Elizabeth airports.

Aviation Forecast and observation are products or value added services of the South African Service.
